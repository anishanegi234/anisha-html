HTML Character Entities Example

Here are some commonly used HTML character entities:

  • < for <
  • > for >
  • & for &
  • " for "
  • ' for ' (single quote)

You can also use numerical character references:

  • < for <
  • > for >
  • & for &
  • " for "
  • ' for ' (single quote)
